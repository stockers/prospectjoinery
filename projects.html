"Attic Storage Doors"

  1. Concealed boiler and large storage area under sloping roof in attic to allow the rest of room to be used as a bedroom. Frame constructed from planed interior wood with studs to accommodate as many opening doors as possible.



    Cut the door panels to accommodate sloping ceiling of attic, 3-4 hinges and handle on each opening door.



    Access to boiler with easily removable studs either side when greater access required, i.e. for boiler maintenance. Access to low-level storage under eaves, with a removable panel in the corner.
